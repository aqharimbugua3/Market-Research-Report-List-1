<p><p>The rod mill market is segmented into two main types: wet grinding and dry grinding. Wet grinding rod mills utilize water to facilitate the grinding process, resulting in finer particles and increased efficiency in mineral processing. They are commonly used in applications requiring precise particle size control. In contrast, dry grinding rod mills operate without water, making them suitable for materials that may react adversely to moisture. This type is typically employed in industries like cement production and aggregate processing.</p></p>
