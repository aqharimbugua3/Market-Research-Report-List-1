<p><p>The Chlorine Dioxide Systems market is categorized into two main methods: electrolytic and chemical. The electrolytic method generates chlorine dioxide through the electrolysis of sodium chloride solutions, offering precise control and lower chemical input. In contrast, the chemical method involves the reaction of sodium chlorite with acids, producing chlorine dioxide on-site. Each method has its advantages; the electrolytic method is typically more environmentally friendly, while the chemical method is often preferred for its simplicity and efficiency in large-scale applications.</p></p>
