<p><p>The Terahertz Imaging Detection Market comprises two primary types: passive and active terahertz imaging. Passive terahertz imaging detects naturally emitted terahertz radiation from objects, making it ideal for applications like security scanning and non-invasive testing. In contrast, active terahertz imaging involves the use of terahertz sources to illuminate objects, enhancing image quality and enabling detailed analysis. Both methods are utilized across various sectors such as healthcare, security, and materials science, each offering unique advantages depending on the application requirements.</p></p>
