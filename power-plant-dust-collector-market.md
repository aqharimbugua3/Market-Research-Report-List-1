<p><p>The Power Plant Dust Collector market encompasses several technologies, including Bag Dust Collectors, Electrostatic Precipitators (ESPs), and Electrostatic-Bag Precipitators (EBPs). Bag Dust Collectors utilize fabric filters to capture particulate matter, offering high efficiency for a range of dust types. ESPs employ electrical fields to remove particles from flue gases, ideal for high-temperature applications. EBPs combine the benefits of both systems, enhancing dust capture efficiency while reducing maintenance. Each type plays a crucial role in improving air quality and meeting regulatory standards in power generation.</p></p>
