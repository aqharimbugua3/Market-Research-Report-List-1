<p><p>Real Time Tracking Systems utilize various technologies for asset and personnel location tracking. RFID employs electromagnetic fields for short-range identification, while Wi-Fi leverages existing network infrastructure for broader coverage. Ultrasound uses sound waves for accurate indoor positioning. Infrared relies on light waves for proximity detection. ZigBee is ideal for low-power, short-range communication among devices. Ultra-Wide Band (UWB) provides precise spatial tracking over short distances. Other market solutions may include GPS, Bluetooth, and combination systems tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
