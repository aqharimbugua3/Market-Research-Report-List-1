<p><p>The hydro turbine runner market consists of various types, including Francis, Kaplan, Pelton, and others. Francis turbines are versatile and suited for medium head applications, utilizing a radial flow design. Kaplan turbines excel in low head sites with adjustable blades, enhancing efficiency. Pelton turbines operate efficiently at high heads, using impulse mechanisms to harness kinetic energy from water jets. Other types include cross-flow and bulb turbines, catering to specific hydropower applications, contributing to the diverse landscape of hydro turbine technology.</p></p>
