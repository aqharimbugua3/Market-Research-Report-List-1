<p><p>The shortwave radio market is categorized into three main types: FM, FM/AM, and Others. FM radios primarily transmit signals over frequency modulated bands, offering high sound quality and less interference. FM/AM radios combine both frequency and amplitude modulation, allowing for diverse broadcasting options and wider reach. The "Others" category includes digital and internet-connected radios, which provide enhanced features like streaming and additional channel access. Each type serves varied user preferences and listening environments, shaping the shortwave radio landscape.</p></p>
