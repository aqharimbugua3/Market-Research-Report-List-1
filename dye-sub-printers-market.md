<p><strong>The Dye Sub Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ReTransfer</li><li>Direct to Card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dye sublimation printers serve various market types, primarily categorized into retransfer and direct-to-card. Retransfer printers involve a two-step process where images are first printed onto a transfer film, which is then applied to the card surface, offering superior image quality and durability. In contrast, direct-to-card printers print directly onto the card material, providing quicker results and a more compact design. Both types cater to distinct needs in ID card production, access control, and personalizations, each with its specific advantages.</p></p>
