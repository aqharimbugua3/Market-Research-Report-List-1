<p><p>The probe station market consists of three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic probe stations. Manual probe stations require operators to manually position probes for testing, offering high flexibility but lower throughput. Semi-automatic probe stations provide some automation features, improving efficiency while still requiring operator input. Automatic probe stations fully automate the probing process, significantly increasing testing speed and repeatability, making them ideal for high-volume production environments. Each type caters to different testing needs and production scales.</p></p>
