<p><ul><li>DEXA Technology</li><li>NIR Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Meat Fat Analyzer market comprises two primary technologies: DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) and NIR (Near-Infrared Spectroscopy). DEXA technology utilizes low-level X-rays to accurately measure fat content in meat products, providing precise data for quality control. In contrast, NIR technology employs infrared light to analyze the composition of meat surfaces, offering rapid and non-destructive results. Both methods serve crucial roles in enhancing meat quality assurance and optimizing product formulations in the food industry.</p></p>
