<p><p>The centrifugal separator market is primarily divided into two types: liquid-liquid separation and liquid-solid separation. Liquid-liquid separators efficiently separate immiscible liquids with different densities, commonly used in oil-water separation and wastewater treatment. Liquid-solid separators, on the other hand, focus on removing solid particles from liquids, often employed in industries like food processing and mining. Both types leverage centrifugal force to enhance separation efficiency, addressing specific needs across various sectors and contributing to operational effectiveness.</p></p>
