<p><p>Voltage Dependent Resistors (VDRs) are components that change their resistance based on the applied voltage, primarily used for surge protection and voltage clamping in electronic circuits. The market for VDRs is divided into two main types: SMD (Surface-Mount Device) and Leaded types. SMD VDRs are designed for automated assembly and occupy less space, making them ideal for compact devices. Leaded types, with protruding leads, are suited for through-hole mounting, offering easier handling and robust electrical connections in various applications.</p></p>
