<p><strong>The VOC Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC (Volatile Organic Compounds) detector market primarily includes two main types: PID (Photoionization Detectors) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID detectors utilize ultraviolet light to ionize gas particles, providing rapid and accurate measurements of VOC concentrations, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, MOS sensors detect changes in electrical resistance when exposed to VOCs, offering a cost-effective and compact solution for indoor air quality monitoring in residential and commercial settings. Both technologies play crucial roles in ensuring environmental safety.</p></p>
